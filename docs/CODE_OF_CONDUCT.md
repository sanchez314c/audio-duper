## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of:

- **Experience Level** - From beginners to experts
- **Background** - Personal, educational, or professional
- **Identity** - Gender, gender identity, age, sexual orientation
- **Culture** - Race, ethnicity, nationality, religion
- **Ability** - Physical or mental abilities
- **Expression** - Communication style and language

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

#### ‚úÖ Positive Behaviors

- **Respectful Communication**
  - Use welcoming and inclusive language
  - Be respectful of differing viewpoints
  - Accept constructive criticism gracefully
  - Focus on what is best for the community

- **Collaborative Spirit**
  - Work together to solve problems
  - Help others learn and grow
  - Share knowledge openly
  - Mentor and support newcomers

- **Professional Conduct**
  - Be considerate and professional
  - Follow project guidelines
  - Respect project maintainers' time
  - Contribute constructively to discussions

- **Inclusive Environment**
  - Welcome newcomers and help them get started
  - Be patient with questions and learning curves
  - Use preferred pronouns and names
  - Avoid assumptions about others' backgrounds

#### ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**
  - Unwelcome comments about identity or appearance
  - Deliberate intimidation
  - Stalking or following
  - Unwanted sexual attention

- **Discriminatory Language**
  - Slurs or derogatory language
  - Stereotypes or generalizations
  - Exclusionary jokes or humor
  - Microaggressions

- **Disruptive Behavior**
  - Spam or excessive messaging
  - Off-topic discussions in inappropriate venues
  - Deliberate disruption of conversations
  - Trolling or flame wars

- **Privacy Violations**
  - Sharing private information without consent
  - Recording conversations without permission
  - Doxxing or revealing personal details
  - Publishing private communications

## Enforcement Process

- **48 Hours**: Investigation begins
- **1 Week**: Resolution for clear cases
- **2 Weeks**: Complex cases resolved

### Enforcement Actions

Based on severity and context:

#### üü° First Offense

- **Private Warning** - Direct message from maintainers
- **Education** - Resources on appropriate behavior
- **Request for Apology** - If appropriate

#### üü† Second Offense

- **Public Warning** - Moderated comment in relevant space
- **Temporary Restrictions** - Limited interaction privileges
- **Required Acknowledgment** - Must understand guidelines

#### üî¥ Third Offense

- **Temporary Ban** - 1 week to 1 month
- **Public Statement** - Community notification
- **Appeal Process** - Opportunity to respond

#### üö´ Severe Violations

- **Immediate Ban** - Permanent removal from community
- **Legal Action** - If laws were broken
- **Platform Reporting** - To GitHub or authorities as needed

### Appeal Process

1. **Submit Appeal** - To maintainers within 7 days
2. **Review** - All maintainers review the case
3. **Decision** - Final decision within 14 days
4. **Documentation** - Outcome recorded for transparency

## Community Guidelines

### Communication Standards

#### In Issues and PRs

- **Stay on topic** - Focus on the technical matter
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful feedback
- **Assume good faith** - Give benefit of doubt
- **Use inclusive language** - Avoid gendered or cultural assumptions

#### In Discussions

- **Welcome newcomers** - Help them feel included
- **Share knowledge** - Answer questions patiently
- **Avoid gatekeeping** - Don't create barriers to entry
- **Celebrate contributions** - Recognize good work

#### In Code Reviews

- **Focus on code, not person** - Technical feedback only
- **Explain reasoning** - Help others understand
- **Suggest improvements** - Don't just point out problems
- **Be encouraging** - Acknowledge good practices

### Project-Specific Guidelines

#### AudioDUPER Community Values

1. **Privacy First**
   - Respect user data concerns
   - Advocate for secure practices
   - Question data collection features

2. **Accessibility Focus**
   - Consider users with disabilities
   - Test with accessibility tools
   - Include alt text and descriptions

3. **Performance Awareness**
   - Optimize for various hardware
   - Consider network conditions
   - Test on older systems

4. **Cross-Platform Respect**
   - Value all operating systems equally
   - Avoid platform-specific assumptions
   - Test on multiple platforms
